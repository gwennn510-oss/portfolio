Academic Abstract Translation — Master's Thesis

Chinese → Bahasa Malaysia · University Submission Standard

Translated a Master's thesis abstract on classical Chinese drama into Bahasa Malaysia, meeting Malaysian university requirements that mandate a Malay-language abstract regardless of the thesis's primary language. The subject matter — an analysis of the theatrical technique "play within a play" in the works of Qing dynasty dramatist Jiang Shiquan — required precise handling of literary-critical terminology across two typologically distinct language systems, while preserving academic register and argumentative clarity throughout.

Source — Chinese

"戏中用戏"作为一种传统的戏剧创作手法，能够营造出草蛇灰线、精妙入神的戏剧效果，为众多戏剧作家所运用。作为清中期戏曲界的"殿军"，蒋士铨（1725-1785）也同样擅长在自己的戏剧作品当中使用这种艺术手法。本文旨在通过对其戏剧作品中"戏中用戏"手法进行分析，深入理解蒋士铨的戏曲主张，探究其作品的叙事艺术与创作原则。

Translation — Bahasa Malaysia

Teknik "lakonan dalam lakonan" merupakan cara pengaryaan drama tradisional yang sering digunakan oleh penulis drama. Sebagai seorang penulis drama yang terkenal pada zaman pertengahan Dinasti Qing, Jiang Shiquan (1725–1785) juga mahir mengaplikasikan teknik ini dalam karyanya. Kajian ini bertujuan untuk mengkaji dengan lebih mendalam proposisi drama Jiang Shiquan serta meneliti gaya naratif dan prinsip penciptaan karyanya melalui analisis teknik "lakonan dalam lakonan" dalam karya dramanya.

Source — Chinese

东亚大陆与南亚次大陆板块的运动和挤压，造就了号称"世界屋脊"的喜马拉雅山脉；漫长的社会文明嬗递过程中，物质世界和精神活动的震荡与催化，诞生了一位被誉为"一代兵圣"的孙武。在血与火的搏击中，在智和力的较量里，他呕心沥血，剔精抉微，铸成了一部不朽的兵学巨著——《孙子兵法》。

The vast tectonic movements that forced the East Asian continent against the Indian subcontinent gave rise to the Himalayas, the so-called "Roof of the World." Through the long evolution of human society, marked by upheaval, conflict, and creative ferment, a "Sage of War" emerged — Sun Wu (Sun Tzu). Battling amid the chaos of blood and fire, he devoted his life to probing the essence of warfare, leaving a masterpiece of military strategy: The Art of War.

Di medan pertempuran darah dan api, beliau mencurahkan seluruh tenaga dan ilmunya untuk menghasilkan sebuah karya agung ilmu ketenteraan — The Art of War (Seni Peperangan).

Localization & Subtitling

Animation Localization — Loyal Hero (精忠少年岳飞)

Chinese → English · 13 Episodes · Children's Series

Localized a 13-episode Chinese historical animation (~10 min/ep) into English for international audiences. Required independent historical research — verifying period-accurate terminology and cultural references from the Song dynasty context. A key editorial challenge was character-title consistency: the same person addressed differently depending on speaker and context, requiring a clear termbase maintained across all 13 episodes.

Sample — Narration (Third-Person)

起初周同只教文不授武At first, Zhou Tong taught books, not fighting.
众兄弟深夜遭遇狼群One night, the boys were attacked by wolves.
终得师父解救But their master saved them.
先教忠义立根本 / 再淬刀锋成将才He taught them what was right first, before teaching them how to fight.

Sample — Dialogue (Direct Address)

这位就是咱们的师父This is our master.
大侠周同The great hero, Zhou Tong.
周同不过如此Zhou Tong is nothing special.
你这伤，本王留的 / 是你一辈子的荣耀That scar I gave you? The greatest honor you'll have!

Editorial Note — Terminology

Termbase: 师父 → "Master" (direct address); 周同 → "Zhou Tong" (narration); 大侠周同 → "The great hero, Zhou Tong" (formal introduction). All lines finalized to director CPS limits in simple English for ages 7–12.
